Courses Offered

B.Tech

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree programme awarded to candidates after the completion of four years of study in the field. Engineering is one of the most popular courses in India and there are many institutes that offer the course to aspiring students

Pharmacy

A Graduate degree in Pharmacy offers a vast number of rewarding career opportunities in the Health-care sector. Indian Pharmaceutical Industry is a rapidly growing sector, second only to IT, with a 15% annual growth rate and exports crossing Rs.15,000 crores. Annual demand of pharmacist is increasing in leaps and bounds.

MBA

A Master of Business Administration, or MBA, is a general graduate business degree that teaches students technical, managerial, and leadership skills. Earning an MBA gives you valuable business acumen, expands your professional network, and creates new opportunities.
